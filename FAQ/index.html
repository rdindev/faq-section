Frequently Asked Questions
1. Is the event exclusive to Jadavpur University Students ?

The good news is that, Hello IEEE is open to students from all colleges and universities who wish to be a part of this oneof-a-kind global event.
2. Can I participate alone? If not, then what should be the team size?

No, you cannot participate in the events alone. The team should consist of maximum three members.
3. Can I participate in both the quiz and the idea presentation events?

Yes, you can participate in both the events. But if you get qualified for the Finale of either of them, you will have to forfeit the other one.
4.What will I receive if I participate?


Refreshments (or lunch) will be provided to all the attendees, besides a certificate of participation. Winners have enticing prizes in store!
